The Challenge

Critical Issues in Blood Management

Healthcare organizations worldwide face significant operational challenges that cost lives and resources every day.

Artificial Shortages

Over-reservation by hospitals creates phantom shortages while blood sits unused elsewhere.

Blood Wastage

Expired blood units due to poor tracking and coordination between facilities.

Poor Coordination

Lack of real-time visibility between hospitals and blood banks hampers efficiency.

Delayed Emergency Response

Critical patients wait while staff manually search for available blood units.

Inefficient Lifecycle Tracking

Manual processes make it difficult to track blood units from collection to transfusion.

No Centralized Visibility

Health authorities lack real-time data to make informed decisions and allocations.

Platform Features

Comprehensive Blood Management Solution

RaktSetu provides end-to-end visibility and control over blood inventory across your healthcare network.

Real-Time Inventory Visibility

Monitor blood units across all connected facilities in real-time. Track availability by blood group, component type, and location instantly.

Time-Bound Reservations

Automatic release of over-reserved units back to inventory. Prevent artificial shortages with smart reservation management.

Near-Expiry Alerts

Proactive notifications for blood units approaching expiry. Reduce wastage through intelligent redistribution suggestions.

Centralized Tracking

Complete blood lifecycle visibility from collection to transfusion. Audit trails for compliance and quality assurance.

Emergency Coordination

Priority handling for emergency requests. Instant matching with available inventory across the network.

Analytics & Reporting

Comprehensive dashboards and reports for data-driven decisions. Track usage patterns, wastage metrics, and efficiency KPIs.
